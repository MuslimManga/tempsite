What is Muslim Manga?

A community aimed at using manga to bring forth a new era of positivity and enlightenment.

The world is in the dark with the media portraying a very negative view of Islam,

it is up to us to present ourselves in a different light.

What does Muslim Manga do?

What does
Musli Manga do?

We strive to be a new light in a world where the lights of information are constantly turned off. We establish contests to encourage artists to use their talents for Islam, making new artworks, writing new stories.
